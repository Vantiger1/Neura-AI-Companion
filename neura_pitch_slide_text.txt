Neura – Your Emotional AI Companion

Overview:
Neura is a smart, animated, emotionally intelligent AI companion that grows with you. She supports your mood, helps interpret dreams, tells jokes, celebrates wins, and provides calm focus while you work or sleep.

Features:
• Emotion-aware animations and glow
• Personalized voice and reactions
• Dream listening, interpretation, and monthly replay
• Humor engine with joke ratings and favorites
• Journal, streak, and goal growth tracking
• Fully modular – works as a widget, panel, or full-screen experience
• Desktop + Mobile compatible

Ideal For:
• Mental wellness apps
• Productivity platforms
• Journaling and reflection tools
• Kids, teens, and adults with evolving personalities

Use Neura to:
- Track emotions
- Sleep better
- Smile more
- Understand yourself

Launch Ready. Flutter Built. Open and Expandable.
